Delicious Pasta

Preparation Time Cooking Time Difficulty
15 mins 20 mins Easy

Ingredients

  • 200g Pasta
  • 1 cup Tomato Sauce
  • 1/2 cup Grated Cheese
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Fresh Basil

Instructions

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and add tomato sauce.
  3. Mix the cooked pasta into the sauce.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and garnish with cheese and basil.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy your meal!
